About Us

Our Story

Around the House is more than just a business; it's a continuation of a family legacy and a dream shared by two cousins, John Borg and Damien Granger. What started as a remodeling company founded by John’s father has evolved into a versatile home maintenance service. After the passing of John’s father, we decided to honor his memory by transitioning the business from remodeling to pressure washing and home care services.

Originally named "Pressure Bros," our business quickly grew, and after discovering an old flyer from the original company, we rebranded to "Around the House" to reflect the wide range of services we offer today.

Our Team

As a small, family-operated business, John and Damien handle every aspect of the company themselves:

  • John Borg: Website development, branding, and customer relations.
  • Damien Granger: Social media management and marketing.

We share the physical labor equally, ensuring that every job is completed with the highest standards of quality and care.

Our Mission

We understand how overwhelming home maintenance can be. Our mission is to help those who are unable to keep up with tasks around the house, whether due to time constraints, physical limitations, or simply not knowing where to start. We provide transparent, no-surprise quotes, utilizing tools like Google Earth to measure properties and offer accurate estimates over the phone or via email.

Our services range from pressure washing and house cleaning to junk removal, lawn care, and more. If it’s a job around the house, we’re here to help.

Client Relationships

Building lasting relationships with our clients is at the heart of what we do. Many of our clients have become like family to us, often inviting us over for dinner after a job well done. We believe in rewarding loyalty, which is why we offer discounts and even free services to our most dedicated customers.

Looking Ahead

As we look to the future, our goal is to expand our reach and become a trusted outlet for others who share our passion for honest, fulfilling work. We aim to make a positive impact in every community we serve, connecting with people and building lasting relationships through the services we provide.
