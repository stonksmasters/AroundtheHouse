About Us

Our Story

Around the House is more than just a business; it's a continuation of a family legacy. Founded by cousins John Borg and Damien Granger, the company started as a remodeling business under John’s father. After his passing, we rebranded and expanded to offer comprehensive services, including junk removal and moving services, honoring his memory by transforming homes across Louisiana.

Originally known as "Pressure Bros," we discovered an old flyer from the original company and rebranded to "Around the House" to reflect our broader range of services. We’re proud to serve our local community with dedication, care, and a commitment to excellence.

Our Mission & Values
