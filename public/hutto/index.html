About Us

Our Story

Around the House is more than just a business; it's a continuation of a family legacy. Founded by cousins John Borg and Damien Granger, we began as a remodeling business under John’s father. After he passed, we rebranded to honor his memory and expanded into furniture assembly and pressure washing.

Originally known as "Pressure Bros," we chose "Around the House" to reflect our wider range of services. We serve our community with dedication, care, and excellence.
