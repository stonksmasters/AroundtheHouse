About Us

Our Story

Around the House is more than just a business; it's a continuation of a family legacy. Founded by cousins John Borg and Damien Granger, the company started as a remodeling business under John’s father. After his passing, we rebranded and expanded to offer comprehensive services, including junk removal and moving services, honoring his memory by transforming homes across Louisiana.

Originally known as "Pressure Bros," we discovered an old flyer from the original company and rebranded to "Around the House" to reflect our broader range of services. We’re proud to serve our local community with dedication, care, and a commitment to excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve in Plaquemine?

We proudly serve Plaquemine and the surrounding areas, including nearby neighborhoods and suburbs. If you’re unsure whether we serve your area, feel free to contact us for more details.

How much does pressure washing cost in Plaquemine?

Pricing depends on the size of the area that needs to be cleaned and the type of service. We provide free, no-obligation quotes based on your specific needs. Contact us with your address, and we'll measure the area using Google Earth to give you an accurate estimate.

What kind of equipment do you use?

We use professional-grade John Deere 4000 psi and 4GPM pressure washers, along with environmentally safe cleaning solutions. Our equipment is powerful enough to handle tough grime but gentle enough to protect surfaces, especially older homes and delicate materials.

Do you offer services beyond pressure washing?

Absolutely! In addition to pressure washing, we offer a variety of services including house cleaning, junk removal, lawn care, and general labor around the house. Whether you need your yard cleaned up or help with home organization, we’re here to help.

How often should I have my home pressure washed?

For homes in Plaquemine, we recommend pressure washing at least once a year due to the region’s humidity, which can lead to mold and mildew buildup. If you have lots of trees or live near water, you might consider more frequent cleanings.

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes, we offer discounts for repeat customers, senior citizens, and military personnel. We also have seasonal promotions, so be sure to ask about any current offers when you contact us.

What is your service guarantee?

We stand behind our work 100%. If you’re not satisfied with the results, we’ll come back and make it right at no additional cost. Your satisfaction is our top priority.
